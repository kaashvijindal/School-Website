Whats New

  • MULTIMODAL LEARNING

    To perfect what you’re learning, you have to learn it in more than one way’ We teach our children different subjects and topics with various approaches, that go beyond the convention teaching methodology such as textbooks and tasks. We believe that the most appropriate way of teaching is to perfect it by transferring the knowledge using diverse interactive methods such as computer/internet, worksheets, projects, audio notes, plays, videos and more.

  • VALUE BASED LEARNING

    ‘Values are what we live by’ At Phoenix Greens, we foster our children with values that reflect in them in ways such as compassion, humanity, friendly, moral conduct and respect for others. Our children volunteer to participate in various social service activities, environment protection and much more. AP Vana Mahotsav, Green Building Congress at Novotel, visiting orphanages and old age-homes are just a glimpse of it. Every year, the school raises funds through services and parents’ supports to donate them into charities.
